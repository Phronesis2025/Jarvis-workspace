Create the scheduled task from an elevated PowerShell window:

schtasks /create /tn "Jarvis Overnight Health Check" /tr "powershell.exe -ExecutionPolicy Bypass -File C:\dev\jarvis-workspace\scripts\run_overnight_health_check.ps1" /sc daily /st 02:00

To run it immediately:
schtasks /run /tn "Jarvis Overnight Health Check"

To view it:
schtasks /query /tn "Jarvis Overnight Health Check" /v /fo list

To delete it later:
schtasks /delete /tn "Jarvis Overnight Health Check" /f
